## Process Checkpoints

| Step | Process | Main Structural Change |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | NWell formation | N-type body region |
| 2 | Gate oxidation | Thin gate oxide |
| 3 | Poly deposition | Gate material deposition |
| 4 | Gate patterning | Gate geometry definition |
| 5 | LDD implant | Shallow p-type extension |
| 6 | Spacer deposition | Nitride coating |
| 7 | Spacer etch | Sidewall spacer |
| 8 | S/D implant | p+ source/drain |
| 9 | RTA | Dopant activation and diffusion |
| 10 | Al deposition | Metal layer |
| 11 | Al etch | Electrode pattern |
| 12 | Reflect | Symmetric device structure |
| 13 | Contact definition | Final electrical device |

The final device contains NWell, p-type LDD, p+ source/drain, gate oxide, poly gate, spacer, metal, and contacts.

**Summary:**  
The TDR checkpoints show how the pMOS structure is built through the full process flow.
